Hi! My name is Lisa.

A little about me...

  1. If you could travel anywhere, where would you like to go?
    I would love to be able to travel to hit all the National parks throughout American with my family- maybe even an RV trip if I wasn't driving. There is such a diverse landscape here in our proverbially backyard which I don't feel like I have even begun to explore. The kids got sleeping bags and mats for their birthdays this year and after things are opened up and travel is safer I would love to be able to have an epic American adventure from sea to shining sea.

  2. What is the best advice you've ever been given?
    My mom always spoke to me growing up about fostering balance in your life. This is such a great life lesson and skill to strive for between all the daily demands of work, family and home life.

  3. What do you do for fun?
    I am an avid walker and love to coax my kids into joining me so I can chat them up and check in with them while on the move. I have found this is a great time to be active and also have individual time with each of my three kiddos. I also consider myself an emerging gardener. I love to get dirt under my fingernails, gloves just don't have the same feel, and see the fruits of a physically demanding day outside. My goal is to have a English cottage style perennial garden and each year I try to split what I have and look for local finds to expand my beds with around the yard.

  4. What is your favorite movie or book?
    My favorite books of all time are the Harry Potter series. I loved reading them in college when they first came out and I remember having a hard time putting them down and being captivated by the characters and storyline. Then as a parent I got to experience them through my children as we enjoyed them as a family at night before bedtime. They love them and reference them with the same fondness as I do.

  5. What is the best advice you can give your clients?
    Celebrate each success and praise small victories as you work towards a larger goal.
