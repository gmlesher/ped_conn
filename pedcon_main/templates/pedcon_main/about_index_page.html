Occupational Therapists specialize in helping children succeed in activities of daily living in the home, school, and community. They are uniquely trained to help children detect, interpret, and regulate sensory information which, in turn, allows for appropriate motor and behavioral responses.

All therapists at Pediatric Connections have training in Masgutova Neurosensorimotor Reflex Integration (MNRI) and are the leading local experts in the Masgutova Method. This specialized technique sets us apart from others and offers our clients a highly effective, evidence-based intervention. While we primarily utilize this methodology, we continue to implement other strategies to meet the unique needs of each client.
