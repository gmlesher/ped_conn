Welcome to Pediatric Connections

What We Do

Our Occupational Therapists and Speech Therapists specialize in helping children and adults succeed in activities of daily living in the home, school and community. They are uniquely trained to help detect, interpret, and regulate sensory information which, in turn, allows for appropriate motor and behavioral responses.

Identify

We will identify foundational problems that are effecting daily life.

Understand

We will help you understand how problems impact all aspects of learning, social skills and behavior.

Empower

We will empower you with resources, tools, and strategies for success.
